Replace your utensils with more sustainable ones

Changing the plastic cutlery to biodegradable in order to reduce plastic waste
Biodegradable cutlery

The cutlery change is a project whose goal is to reduce the plastic waste produced by the utensils at the school's cafeteria. It proposes changing the plastic utensils into utensils using biodegradable materials in order to have actions on sustainability at the college.

This change will not only benefit us, but also the planet !

  • 1How can you make a change? Sign the petition by clicking the image on the right !
  • 2When? As soon as possible!
  • 3Why? Because it's a simple but effective change that requires very little effort.
  • 4Collective impact: As a community, it will help us take care of the planet on a regular basis, while also being able to enjoy food from the cafeteria.
