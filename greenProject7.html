Green Team Activities
Green Team Activities

The goal of Champlain's Green Team club is to promote sustainability throughout the semester. It proposes a lot of activities and encourages students to participate in them!

  • 1April 21st: Clean Up Patrol for Earth Day: A special event to commemorate Earth Day in collaboration with the South Shore University Women’s Club, the GoGreen Committee, and the Champlain community! Come declutter, or take a stroll, at Seaway Park with us from 12 p.m. to 1 p.m. on April 21st!
  • 2April 27th: Canvas making out of recycled paper: The Green Team invites you to give new life to old paper and make your very own canvas to paint next week.
  • 3May 4th: Painting on canvas made from reused materialsFollowing last week’s activity, the canvases made of recycled paper are now dry and ready to paint!
