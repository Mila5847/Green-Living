Basketball brings Sustainability to life!

A Ball for the Planet
A Ball for the Planet

This project brings people together. It entertains the public as well as acquiring money for helping the world to be more sustainable. The donations gained will go towards environmental foundations or a team that needs to fund their Sustainability project. In order to get donations, William Tsafack, Marie-Anne Ramsay, Samuel Zoayter Da Rocha, Rohan Arineau, Bassam Bassam, and Ethan Lam came up with the idea to make a basketball contest that will cost only 5$ to be able to view the show. The participants will be the D1 basketball teams from Champlain. Other than that, many other items will be available for the cost of 1$. In the end, this contest is something that you should attend for your simple entertainment and fun.

  • 1Where? Free block, April 27th. Tickets will be sold at the cafeteria for 5$.
  • 2When? During the month of April, when Sustainability week is taking place.
  • 3Why?Donations would be really appreciated in order to help our environment.
  • 4Collective impact: In today's society, it is important to remember to help sustainable organizations.
