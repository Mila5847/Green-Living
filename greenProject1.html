Clothing Donation Drive

The thrift drive is a project whose goal is to help those in need of clothes. A designated box will be located near the cafeteria, where students will be able to donate clothes they no longer need. Students will have the month of April to particiate in this sustainable activity..

The donated clothes will go to .............

  • 1Where? Leave your clothes in the box near the cafeteria.
  • 2When? During the month of April.
  • 3Why? The collected clothes will be really appreciated by those who need them.
  • 4Collective impact: As a community, it is important to learn to help each other.....
