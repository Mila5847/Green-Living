Clothing Donation Drive
Clothing Donation Drive

The thrift drive is a project organized by Camille Girardin, ................. Their goal is to help those in need of clothes. A designated box will be located near the cafeteria, where students will be able to donate clothes they no longer need. Students will have the month of April to particiate in this sustainable activity.

The donated clothes will go to an organization called Big Brothers Big Sisters. For more information on this organization, please click on the image on the right.

  • 1Where? Leave your clothes in the box near the cafeteria.
  • 2When? During the month of April.
  • 3Why? The collected clothes will be really appreciated by those who need them.
  • 4Collective impact: As a community, it is important to learn to help each other and to gro together. Sharing our possessions is a practice that helps us get closer to each other.
