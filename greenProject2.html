Crewdle
Crewdle

The intention behind this action project is to make Champlain College switch from Zoom to Crewdle. Crewdle is an application that produces no carbon emissions compared to Zoom thanks to the usage of peer-to-peer networking. This technology allows people to be able to connect to each other without the use of a third-party (servers). Furthermore, this new innovation encrypts all of the calls which prevents a leak of data and privacy. Crewdle also offers better image quality and reduces the amount of latency, the delay, in meetings.
