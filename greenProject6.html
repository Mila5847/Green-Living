Food Drive for the Less Deprived
Food Drive

Our goal is to help people who do not have the same luck as we have. At the same time, food is often wasted when it is still good. We wanted to achieve two things at once by helping those who are in financial difficulties, as well as putting excess goods to use instead of putting them in the garbage. A raffle will be organized for the donators of this food drive. For every three items donated, the donator will receive a raffle ticket, which will lock in your spot for a draw at the end of the food collection. All given goods have the same value. The importance is to give three non-perishable foods to get one ticket. There will be three raffle winners. A person cannot win more than once. The winners will be contacted through MIO to get their prize. This project is organized by Faisal Tuffaha, Benjamin Wu, Adam Belabbas, Mayar Rekmani, Loïc Chen K. P..
